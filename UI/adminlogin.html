Admin account creation

The creation of administrative accounts follows a stringent protocol where individuals are precluded from generating such accounts for themselves. Instead, the initiation of an admin account necessitates the intervention of an existing admin, who is vested with the authority to perform this crucial task. This deliberate restriction is designed to uphold security measures and prevent unauthorized access, ensuring that only authorized personnel with the requisite privileges can create and manage admin accounts.
